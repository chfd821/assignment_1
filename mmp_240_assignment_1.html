I’m a Multimedia Programming and Design student at BMCC on a mission to use my skills of storytelling—that is distinct to my culture. I hope to use mixed media and visual communication to tell the kinds of stories that drive folks to action. When I’m not staring at a screen, I’m likely being helped by my two cats, who take their jobs as my creative directors very seriously. Most importantly, I’m finally back to my first loves of reading and writing; after a long hiatus from the page, I’m back and realizing that whether it’s code or prose, it’s all just storytelling in different languages.
